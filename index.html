Agent · order-retention-flow

AI Order Processing & Customer Retention System

Filters incoming emails to catch new orders, then uses AI to extract order details from unstructured text and match each item against a pricing database — calculating subtotals, totals, and profit automatically.

Confirmed orders get a unique Order ID, an automated confirmation email with upsell suggestions, and a logged row in Google Sheets (customer, order details, date, cost, total payment, profit), with duplicate-confirmation filtering so the same order never gets processed twice.

A few hours later, the system follows up with a 1–5 star feedback request linked to a pre-filled Tally form, filters duplicate submissions, and logs verified responses back to Sheets. Based on the rating, it sends one of several tailored replies — from thank-you notes to apology and recovery messages — then, a day after that, sends a personalized reorder invitation shaped by how the customer rated their experience.

Tracker · google-sheets

Client Expense & Income Tracker

A professional financial tracker built in Google Sheets to help small business owners and freelancers monitor their monthly cash flow without manual calculation. Every transaction is logged once — the sheet handles totals, summaries, and charts automatically.

Features dropdown data validation for clean consistent entries, conditional formatting that color-codes transaction status at a glance, and SUMIFS formulas that automatically separate and total income vs expenses by month — all feeding into a visual dashboard with summary boxes and a bar chart showing financial performance instantly.
