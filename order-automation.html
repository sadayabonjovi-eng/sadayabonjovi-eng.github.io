Overview

This automation system handles customer orders, tracks data, collects feedback, and sends retention emails to boost repeat business.

Features

  • Filters incoming emails to detect customer orders
  • AI extracts order details naturally — no exact wording required
  • Matches orders against pricing database and calculates subtotals and totals
  • Duplicate order detection to prevent double recording
  • Logs structured data into Google Sheets with cost and profit tracking
  • Sends automated order confirmations with upsell suggestions
  • AI sentiment analysis on customer feedback — generates personalized responses based on positive, neutral or negative ratings
  • Retention automation — follow‑up email 24 hours after feedback

System Flow

  1. Email trigger → detect new orders
  2. AI extraction → parse order details naturally
  3. Menu matching → calculate subtotals and grand total
  4. Confirmation email → sent with upsell suggestions
  5. Google Sheets logging → track costs and profits
  6. Feedback loop → collect ratings via Tally survey
  7. AI sentiment analysis → personalized response based on rating
  8. Retention email → re‑engage customers after 24 hours
