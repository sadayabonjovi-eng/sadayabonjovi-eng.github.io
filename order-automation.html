🛒 AI Order & Customer Retention System

An end‑to‑end automation workflow that processes orders, collects feedback, and re‑engages customers using AI + Zapier + Google Sheets.

Home Projects About Me Contact

📌 Problem

Businesses often struggle with manual order handling, slow feedback collection, and lack of consistent customer follow‑up. This leads to errors, missed opportunities, and reduced retention.

💡 Solution

I designed an AI‑powered automation system that:

  • Order Processing: Filters incoming emails, extracts order details, calculates totals, and sends confirmations.
  • Data Tracking: Logs orders into Google Sheets with Order ID, customer details, cost, and profit.
  • Feedback Automation: Sends rating emails, collects reviews, and generates tailored responses based on satisfaction level.
  • Customer Retention: After 24 hours, automatically sends a follow‑up email inviting customers to order again or visit back.

⚙️ System Flow

  1. Email Trigger: Detects new customer orders.
  2. AI Extraction: Pulls order details from unstructured text.
  3. Google Sheets Logging: Stores structured data for tracking and profit calculation.
  4. Feedback Loop: Sends rating request + logs responses.
  5. Retention Email: Sends personalized re‑engagement after 24 hours.

🚀 Result

This automation reduced manual work, improved accuracy, and increased repeat orders. Customers receive timely confirmations, personalized feedback responses, and re‑engagement emails — strengthening loyalty and boosting profits.
