Overview

This automation system handles customer orders, tracks data, collects feedback, and sends retention emails to boost repeat business.

Features

  • Filters incoming emails to detect customer orders
  • AI extracts order details and calculates totals
  • Logs structured data into Google Sheets
  • Sends automated order confirmations
  • Collects customer feedback with rating system
  • Generates intelligent responses based on ratings
  • Retention automation: follow‑up email after 24 hours

System Flow

  1. Email trigger → detect new orders
  2. AI extraction → parse order details
  3. Google Sheets logging → track costs & profits
  4. Feedback loop → collect ratings & responses
  5. Retention email → re‑engage customers
