Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

// overview

This privacy policy covers all AI agents and automated systems deployed by Bon Jovi F. Sadaya, including the AI Lead Qualification Agent on this portfolio website (sadayabonjovi-eng.github.io), the Automate with Bon Facebook Page Messenger AI Agent, and the Pepper and Amber Facebook Page Messenger AI Agent.

Your privacy is important. This policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected.

// information we collect

When you interact with any of our AI agents, we may collect the following information if you voluntarily provide it during conversation:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • The content of your messages
  • Information about your project or hiring interest
  • Timestamp and source page of your interaction

We do not collect any information without your direct participation in the conversation. No cookies or tracking scripts are used beyond what GitHub Pages provides by default.

// how we use your information

Information collected through our AI agents is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries about services and availability
  • To follow up on potential collaboration or hiring opportunities
  • To send a personalized follow-up email if you expressed interest
  • To improve the quality and accuracy of AI responses

Your information is never sold, shared with third parties for marketing purposes, or used for any purpose beyond direct communication with you.

// data storage

Collected information is logged in a secure Google Sheets document accessible only to Bon Jovi F. Sadaya. Data is retained only as long as necessary to follow up on your inquiry and is not shared with any outside party.

// third party services

Our AI agents use the following third party services, each with their own privacy policies:

Groq AI Cloudflare Workers Make.com Google Apps Script Google Sheets Gmail Meta Messenger API

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these services if you have concerns about how they handle data independently.

// facebook messenger agent

The Automate with Bon Facebook Page Messenger AI Agent is built on the Meta Messenger Platform. When you send a message to the Automate with Bon Facebook Page, Meta processes your message through their platform before it reaches our system. Meta's data handling is governed by their own privacy policy at facebook.com/privacy/policy.

Our Messenger agent only uses your message content to generate a relevant response and to collect your contact details if you express hiring interest — with your voluntary participation.

// your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data collected about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data at any time
  • Opt out of follow-up communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us directly at the email below.
