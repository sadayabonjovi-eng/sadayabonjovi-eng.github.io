// overview

This bot replaces the small daily habit of checking the weather and deciding what to wear or plan around — by doing it automatically, every single morning. At 7 AM sharp, it pulls the day's forecast, hands it to AI to turn into a natural, conversational summary with practical reminders, and sends it straight to the inbox before the day even starts.

It's a small project, but it demonstrates the same core pattern used across all of these automations: trigger → fetch data → process with AI → deliver — a pattern that scales to far more complex business workflows.
